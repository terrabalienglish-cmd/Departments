Why We Exist

You don't have to build your
business alone.

Most founders are figuring it out as they go. Talented, driven, full of ideas — but doing it in isolation. Without honest feedback. Without people who've actually been through it. Without a community that genuinely wants them to win.

Terra exists to change that. Not with courses. Not with consultants. Not with anything for sale. With real founders, real conversations, and a structure built to help people grow.

That's it. That's why we're here.

What Terra Is

A community built on trust, not transactions.

Terra is a free mentorship community for entrepreneurs in Bali. We bring together founders at every stage — people building their first business and people who've built several — and create the conditions for genuine growth.

Our core is the 8-week mentorship program. Real mentors. Real founders. Structured support, honest conversation, and daily accountability. Built by people who've been through it, for people going through it now.

Everything else we build — events, workshops, school sessions, partnerships — exists to support that core purpose. If it doesn't serve the founder, it doesn't belong here.

What Terra Promises

What you can always count on. What will never happen.

  • Always
    Free access for every founder, every time.
    No fees, no upsells, no premium tiers. If you're a founder in Bali, Terra is yours.
  • Always
    Honest mentorship from real founders.
    Our mentors are people who've built things. They share what actually happened, not what sounds good.
  • Always
    A structure you can rely on.
    Clear roles, clear processes, clear expectations. So everyone knows what to do and the community keeps working whether or not any single person is in the room.
  • Always
    Your growth comes first.
    Every decision — what events to run, what speakers to bring, how to structure the program — gets measured against one question: does this actually help founders grow?
  • Never
    No selling inside Terra.
    No one sells anything to community members inside Terra. Not products, not services, not courses. The moment Terra becomes a sales channel, the trust that makes it work disappears.
  • Never
    No gatekeeping.
    Terra doesn't belong to any single person, including the people who built it. It belongs to the community it serves.
  • Never
    No dependency on individuals.
    Terra is designed so that if any one person leaves — including leadership — the community continues. Every role is documented. Every function has a successor being trained. The system holds, not the people.
How We Operate

Four principles that guide every decision.

01
Trust Before Everything
Trust is why Terra works. We protect it in everything we do — how we treat members, how we run events, how we make decisions. When in doubt: does this build or break trust?
02
Structure Is Freedom
Clear structure doesn't slow things down — it frees people up. When everyone knows their role and the process is clear, energy goes into the work instead of figuring out who does what.
03
Everyone Grows Here
Terra isn't just for mentees. Mentors grow. Volunteers grow. Leaders grow. If you're in Terra, Terra is working for you too — not just the people you're helping.
04
The Ecosystem Wins Together
Terra works because everyone in it benefits from everyone else's success. We're not competing. We're compounding. What's good for the community is good for you.
When Things Get Unclear

The question that makes hard decisions easy.

The Terra Decision Test
"Does this help a founder grow — and does it protect the trust that makes Terra work?"
If yes to both — do it.
If no to either — don't.

This applies to every decision in every department. New event format, new partnership, new speaker, new process change. Run it through this test first. It will give you the answer faster than any meeting.
For Everyone on the Terra Team

What it means to lead here.

Every person in a Terra role — department lead, volunteer, mentor, organizer — is not just doing a job. They're building something that helps real people grow their businesses and their lives.

That means your role matters beyond the tasks in it. How you show up, how you treat people, and how you hand things over when you move on — all of it shapes what Terra becomes.

One expectation runs through every role: train the person who comes after you. Not because you're leaving — but because the community is stronger when knowledge lives in the system, not just in you. Every lead trains two successors. That's not a task. That's the culture.

Terra works when everyone in it operates like an owner. Not waiting for permission. Not passing problems up. Taking responsibility for your domain, asking for support when you need it, and keeping the community's purpose at the center of every decision you make.
